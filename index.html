About Us

Nellai College of Engineering, Maruthakulam (formerly National College of Engineering) is an ISO 9001:2015 certified Muslim minority institution approved by AICTE,
New Delhi, and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The college was inaugurated by Her Excellency,
the then Governor of Tamil Nadu, Justice Ms. M. FathimaBeevi on 7th September 2000.
